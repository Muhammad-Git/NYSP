CLIENT FAQS

Who is buying my home?

Name Your Selling Price has developed an expansive nationwide network featuring hundreds of pre-approved real estate cash buyers and investors. We connect you directly with these investors, simplifying the process of selling your home to a qualified buyer.

Do I need an agent to receive a cash offer?

No, you do not need to work with a real estate agent to request an offer with NameYourSellingPrice.

Can I sell a home that’s currently listed?

If you're interested in selling your home with an agent we are here to assist you in finding the ideal agent that suits your preferences.

Do I have to move right away?

Absolutely not. You'll have the freedom to choose a move-in date that aligns with your schedule, with options available up to 30 days after the closing.

Is there a cost to request offers?

No! Requesting offers is completely free and there is no obligation.

Am I obligated to accept an offer?

No, you are not obligated to accept any offers you receive.
